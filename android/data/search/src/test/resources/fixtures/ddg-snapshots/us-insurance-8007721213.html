800-772-1213 Social Security - Should I Answer?
Official Social Security Administration number. However, scammers frequently spoof this number. SSA rarely calls first. If unsure, hang up and call back directly.
SSA Scam Alert | Social Security Administration
Scammers are spoofing 1-800-772-1213 to impersonate Social Security. They threaten benefit suspension or arrest. SSA will never threaten you or demand immediate payment.
Report Social Security Scams - Office of Inspector General
Social Security scam calls have surged. Callers use the official number 800-772-1213 via spoofing. They claim your SSN is suspended. This is fraud.
AARP: Social Security Phone Scam Warning
Number 800-772-1213 is being spoofed by scammers posing as SSA agents. They claim your benefits will be cut off. Mixed reports: some legitimate, some scam.
FTC: Social Security Impersonation is Top Reported Scam
Social Security impersonation is the most reported government scam. Callers spoof 800-772-1213 and claim your SSN is compromised. FTC received 700,000 reports.
